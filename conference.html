International Conference on Instrumentation Signal Processing and Computing Engineering (ICISPACE-2023)

[11th & 12th August, 2023]
(Organized by CVR College of Engineering, Hyderabad)

About the Conference

International Conference on Instrumentation Signal Processing and Computing Engineering (ICISPACE-2023) will provide common platform for presentation of original research findings, exchange of ideas and dissemination of innovative, practical development experiences in different aspects and fields of Instrumentation, signal Processing and Computing. The conference aims at bringing together the researchers, scientists, engineers, industrial professionals and scholar students in their domain of interest. Also, it concentrates on both theory and practices from around the world in all the areas of related disciplines of Instrumentation and efficient Computing. This conference aims to provide rapid dissemination of important results in instrumentation, system design, advance computing technologies, a fusion of research in fuzzy logic, evolutionary computations, neural science and neural network systems and chaos theory and chaotic systems, swarm-based algorithms etc. It promotes novel and high-quality research findings, innovative solutions to challenging Engineering problems; the conference seeks to continuously advance the state-of-the-art in recent developments as well as the other promising areas of advance computing. Original and unpublished research papers from all disciplines including theory, practice, experimentation, and review work highlighting specific research domains for presentation in the technical sessions of the conference will be invited from researchers, academicians and professionals throughout the globe.

Submission Details

The Original Unpublished Research Papers, Articles & Working papers on the topics related to the theme are invited for presentation/publication in the conference proceedings. Full papers in strict Springer AISC format within 8 pages must be submitted online at (ICISPACE) through easy chair paper submission system ICISPACE 2023. The conference proceedings will be published in reputed journal [Indexed by: ISI Proceedings, DBLP. Ulrich's, EI-Compendex, SCOPUS, Zentralblatt Math, Meta Press, Springer link]. Some of the selected high quality papers presented in ICISPACE-2023 with extended versions will be published in some reputed International Peer Reviewed Journals (In process).
